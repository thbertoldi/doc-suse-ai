WHAT?::
  This document focuses on techniques for gathering telemetry data
  from all {productname} components, including metrics, logs and traces.

WHY?::
  To observe, analyze and maintain the behavior, performance and
  health of your {productname} environment, and to troubleshoot issues
  effectively.

EFFORT::
  Setting up the recommended monitoring configurations with {sobservability} is
  straightforward. Advanced setups for more granular control may require
  additional time for specialized analysis and fine-tuning.

GOAL::
  To visualize the complete topology of your services and
  operations, providing deep insights and clarity into your
  {productname} environment.
